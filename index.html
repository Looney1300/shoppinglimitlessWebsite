Captain Steel

Join Captain Steel and his crew upon the mighty Wanderlust on adventures around the world searching for treasure, finding mystical relics, and battling with pirates!

The first of the Captain Steel series, Captain Steel: The First Adventure tells the tale that started all their adventures. Having constructed himself a ship and hired a brave crew, Captain Joshua Steel and his long time friend and first mate Isaac Flint set out to find a treasure and begin their fight against rogue pirates on the high seas.

A tavern owner, Amil Fuego, is rumored to have the information First Mate Flint and Captain Steel need. Through a search for glittering treasure, encounters with a mystical relic, and a battle with pirates, Flint and Steel find that adventures are rarely easy, always exciting, and definitely worth the cost.
